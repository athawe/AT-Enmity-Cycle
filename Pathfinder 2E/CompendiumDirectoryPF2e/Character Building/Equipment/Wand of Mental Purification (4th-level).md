# Wand of Mental Purification (4th-level)
## Item 10
### [[emotion]][[healing]][[magical]][[mental]][[wand]]


**Price** 1400 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

Red feathers hang from the handle of this ivory wand. Holding it brings a sense of gentle calm.

**Activate** Cast a Spell

**Frequency** once per day, plus overcharge

* * *

**Effect** You cast 4th-rank [[Spells/Soothe|Soothe]], and can attempt to counteract one mental effect on the same target. Treat the [[Spells/Soothe|Soothe]] spell's rank as 1 higher for this counteract check.

* * *

**Craft Requirements** Supply a casting of _soothe_ of the appropriate rank.
