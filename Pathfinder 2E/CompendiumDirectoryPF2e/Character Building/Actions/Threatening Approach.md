# Threatening Approach `pf2:2`

### 

**Category** offensive; 




You Stride to be adjacent to a foe and [[Actions/Demoralize|Demoralize]] that foe. If you succeed, the foe is [[Conditions/Frightened 1|Frightened 2]] instead of frightened 1.
